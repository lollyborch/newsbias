About the fact checking process

What are the four criteria that will be used?

  • Is the content factually correct?
  • Have all aspects been presented In a reasonable, even and balanced manner?
  • Is the headline refelctive of the article's content? Does it exaggerate elements that are not central to the issue or focus of the article?
  • How sensational is the tone? Are "loaded" words used?

What are the grade cutoffs

  • A : 95% or greater
  • B : 80-94%
  • C : 68-79%
  • D : 55-67%
  • F : Less than 54%

What categories of news will be checked?

Business, Current Events, Education, Health, Politics, Science / Environment

What categories won't be assessed?

Cars, Entertainment, Lifestyle, Local, Property, Religion / Spirituality, Sports, Technology, Travel, Weather
